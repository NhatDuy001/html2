Food's name: Energy balls

▸ Ingredients:

  • Oat: 80g
  • Honey: 115g
  • Almond Butter: 125g
  • Linseed (55g) or whole chia seeds (110g)
  • Vanilla essence: 5ml
  • Dried fruit or chopped dark chocolate: 80g

▸ Time to prepare: 45 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 large bowl

▸ Detailed recipe:

      - Mix all ingredients in a large (clean) bowl.
      - Form the mixture into small balls and place it in the refrigerator.
      - After 15-30 minutes, you can bring it out and enjoy it.

=> So you've got yourself a delicious and nutritious dish....

  • Energy:190 kcal
  • Fat: 9.1 g
