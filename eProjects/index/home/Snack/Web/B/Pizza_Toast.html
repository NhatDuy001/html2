Food's name: Pizza Toast

▸ Ingredients:

  • Bread: 6 slices
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Chopped mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperonis mini
  • Parsley: 1 tablespoon

▸ Time to prepare: 30 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 6 cakes

▸ Detailed recipe: 

Step 1:
          ↪Preheat oven to 350° and line a medium baking tray with parchment 
        paper. Place the bread on the prepared baking tray and spread the pizza 
        sauce. Coat mozzarella and mini pepperonis on top and place in the oven.     
        Bake until cheese is melted and pepperoni is slightly crispy.
 
       Step 2:
                  ↪Garnish with parmesan and parsley for a nice look.

=>So you can enjoy delicious pizza toast!
