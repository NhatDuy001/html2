Food's name: Stewed chicken with squid fish bone

▸ Ingredients:

  • Chicken: 150g
  • Squid fish bones: 30g
  • Ginger: 2 branches
  • 2 jujubes

▸ Time to prepare: 55 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 4 to 6 servings

▸ Detailed recipe:

     - Wash all ingredients above
     - Chopped jujube, minced ginger, peeled
     - Add jujube, squid fish bones, a few slices of ginger, and seasoning seeds to marinate the chicken.
     - Put the chicken in the casserole with water until the chicken is tender.
     - Season to taste and then turn off the heat.

▸ Calories and related information:

  • Calories: 325

  • Vitamins B12, A, E, C, B1, B2, PP
