Food's name: Dragon fruit soaked in yogurt

▸ Ingredients:

  • Dragon fruit: 1 fruit
  • Yogurt: 1 jar
  • Condensed milk: 1 tbsp

▸ Time to prepare: 15 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium bowl

▸ Detailed recipe:

     - When buying dragon fruit, you peel off the outer layer of thorns, cut it in half, then remove the skin, cut it into bite-sized pieces, and put them on a plate.
     - You add to the plate of dragon fruit 1 jar of yogurt, 1 tablespoon of condensed milk, when mixed, you can enjoy!
     - With just a few simple steps, you have finished the dish of dragon fruit with yogurt, if you like, you can add some shaved ice to make it cooler.
     - Sweet and cool dragon fruit, adding the sweet and sour taste of yogurt and condensed milk is very delicious, show your talent in the kitchen to treat the whole family!
