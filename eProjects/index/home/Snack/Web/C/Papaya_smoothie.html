Food's name: Papaya smoothie

▸ Ingredients:

  • Ripe papaya: 200g
  • Fresh milk: 200ml
  • White sugar

▸ Time to prepare: 20 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium cup

▸ Detailed recipe:

     - Wash papaya, cut into small pieces.
     - Put ripe papaya cut into pieces, milk, sugar, a little ice into the blender, proceed to puree.

▸ Calories and related information:

  • Calories: 139
  • Carbohydrates: 15g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Protein: 1g
  • Potassium, Vitamins C, A, B9
