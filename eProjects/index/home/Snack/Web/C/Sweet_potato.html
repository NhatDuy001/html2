Food's name: Boiled sweet potato

▸ Ingredients:

  • Sweet potato: 1kg

▸ Time to prepare: 30 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 5-7 sweet potatoes

▸ Detailed recipe:

     - Wash sweet potatoes
     - Put the potatoes in the pot, add water until all the potatoes are covered.
     - Cook until the potatoes are soft, then turn off the heat, remove the potatoes to drain.

▸ Calories and related information:

  • Calories: 180
  • Water: 86.8g
  • Protein: 2.49g
  • Fiber: 5.3g
