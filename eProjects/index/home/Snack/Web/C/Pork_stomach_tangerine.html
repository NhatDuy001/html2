Food's name: Pork stomach cooked with tangerines

▸ Ingredients:

  • Pork stomach: 250g
  • Tangerine: 5 fruits
  • Ash powder: 10g
  • Just enough pepper and seasoning

▸ Time to prepare: 60 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium bowl

▸ Detailed recipe:

     - Wash pork belly, cut into long slices.
     - Put tapioca starch, pork stomach, tangerine into a pot and boil over low heat.
     - Cook until the soup is cooked and thickened, seasoning to taste.
     - Use while still hot for best results.
