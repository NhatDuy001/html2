Food's name: Chicken cooked with galangal

▸ Ingredients:

  • Chicken meat: 800g
  • Old galangal: 100g
  • Dangerous chili: 1 fruit
  • Purple onion: 3 pieces
  • Lemongrass: 2 plants
  • Spices: pepper, sugar, fish sauce, cooking oil, soup powder, seasoning seeds

▸ Time to prepare: 45 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: Servings for 4 people

▸ Detailed recipe:

     - Thai only galangal, crushed chili, purple onion, sliced lemongrass.
     - Marinating chicken: Chicken cut into bite-sized pieces, marinated with 2/3 of galangal, lemongrass, 2/3 sliced purple onion, along with spices: 1/2 tablespoon of 
    soup powder, 1/2 teaspoon of pepper, 2 tablespoons seasoning seeds, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 tablespoon fish sauce, 1 tablespoon cooking oil. 
    Mix well to infuse for 20 minutes.
     - Braised chicken: stir-fry the remaining 1/3 of galangal and lemongrass with cooking oil, stir-fry the marinated chicken thoroughly, then add a little water and chili 
    pepper, simmer until the water dries up.
     - Put the chicken on a plate, sprinkle sliced horn chili on top, serve with rice, tomato, cucumber.

=> That's done and now let's enjoy it !
