Food's name: Millet porridge

▸ Ingredients:

  • Millet: 50g
  • Red beans: 30g
  • Peanuts: 50g
  • Alum sugar with the right amount

▸ Time to prepare: 45 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 small bowl

▸ Detailed recipe:

     - Soak all ingredients including red beans, millet, peanuts in water and leave overnight.
     - Remove the ingredients and rinse with clean water.
     - Put the ingredients in a pot containing 1 liter of water, cook on low heat until cooked.
