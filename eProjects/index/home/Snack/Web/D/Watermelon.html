Smoothie name: Watermelon smoothie

▸ Ingredients:

  • Watermelon: 1
  • Sugar milk: 1 box
  • Condensed milk: 2 teaspoons
  • Small ice

▸ Time to prepare: 20 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium-size cup

▸ How to make smoothies: 

     - Watermelon peeled, seeded, cut into small pieces.
     - Then add condensed milk, fresh milk, and crushed ice into the blender.
     - Puree and pour into a glass or cup to enjoy.

▸ Calories and related information:

  • Calories: 30
  • Protein: 0.6 g
  • Carbs: 7.6 g
  • Sugar: 6.2 g
  • Fiber: 0.4 g
  • Fat: 0.2 g
