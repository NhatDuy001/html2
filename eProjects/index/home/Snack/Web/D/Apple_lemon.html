Smoothie name: Apple lemon smoothie

▸ Ingredients:

  • Apple: 1 fruit
  • Lemon: 1 fruit
  • Fresh milk: 30ml fresh milk
  • Honey: 2 teaspoons
  • Small ice

▸ Time to prepare: 15 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium-size cup

▸ How to make avocado smoothie: 

     - Apples are washed, peeled, seeded, and cut into pieces. Cut the lemon in half, remove the seeds, and squeeze out the juice. 
     - Put fresh milk, apple, lemon juice, honey, ice cubes in the blender, pour into a cup and enjoy.
