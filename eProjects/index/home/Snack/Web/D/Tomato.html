Smoothie name: Tomato juice

▸ Ingredients:

  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons of honey
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Small ice

▸ Time to prepare: 15 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium-size cup

▸ How to make avocado smoothie: 

     - Tomatoes are washed, blanched briefly in boiling water for about 2 minutes, then removed, peeled, seeds removed, cut into small pieces. 
     - Then, put tomatoes, fresh milk, honey, lemon juice and a little ice in a blender, add sugar and drink, the cool sweetness will make you excited and passionate.
