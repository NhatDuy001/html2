Smoothie name: Pineapple smoothie

▸ Ingredients:

  • Pineapple : 1/2 fruit
  • Sugar sweetened milk : 200ml
  • Sugar, condensed milk
  • Small ice

▸ Time to prepare: 15 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium-size cup

▸ How to make Pineapple Smoothie: 

     - Pineapple, remove the skin, eyes and cut them into small pieces.
     - Next, you put pineapple, fresh milk, condensed milk, sugar, and crushed ice into the blender.
     - Puree to enjoy. If sweet, you can add more sugar.
