Smoothie name: Jackfruit smoothie

▸ Ingredients:

  • Ripe jackfruit: 500 grams.
  • Fresh milk: 200ml.
  • Condensed milk: 100ml.
  • Yogurt: 2 boxes.
  • Sugar: 3 teaspoons.
  • Small ice.

▸ Time to prepare: 10 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 2 medium-size cup

▸ How to make smoothies: 

     - Buy jackfruit, remove the seeds inside, cut them into small pieces.
     - Next, you put jackfruit, fresh milk, condensed milk, yogurt, and crushed ice into a blender, blend until smooth, 
    then pour into a glass and enjoy.

▸ Calories and related information:

  • Calories: 155
  • Carbs: 40g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Protein: 3g
  • Vitamin A, C
  • Magnesium
