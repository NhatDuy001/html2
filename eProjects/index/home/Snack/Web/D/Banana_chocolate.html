Smoothie name: Chocolate Banana Smoothie

▸ Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas: 2
  • Instant Coffee or Milo or Cocoa Powder: 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh milk: 100ml
  • Small ice

▸ Time to prepare: 15 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 medium-size cup

▸ How to make Chocolate Banana Smoothie: 

     - Peel the banana and cut it into small or thin slices.
     - Then put the mixture of bananas, instant coffee, fresh milk, ice into a blender and puree.
     - When all the ingredients are blended and smooth, pour into a glass to enjoy, you can add more sugar if you want it sweeter.
