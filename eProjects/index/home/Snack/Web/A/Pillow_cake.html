Food's name: Pillow cake

▸ Ingredients:
*Cake part:
  • All-purpose flour: 500g
  • Baking powder: 1.5g
  • Low-sugar fresh milk: 220ml
  • Butter: 50g or 50ml of cooking oil
  • Salt: teaspoon
 *Cake filling:
  • 4-5 pieces of wood ear
  • Beans or kohlrabi: 1/2
  • Purple onion, purple onion: 1/2
  • Seasoning powder, pepper
  • Ground pork: 200g
 *Dipping sauce:
  • Fish sauce: 3 tablespoons
  • Water: 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • MSG: 1 teaspoon
  • Chop chili, garlic, lemon juice.

▸ Time to prepare: 50 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: about 20 pillow cakes

▸ Detailed recipe:

Step 1: Make the pillow cake:

     - Butter needs to be melted first. Put 220ml of fresh milk, yeast, 2 eggs, salt, and pre-heated butter in a bowl, stir well and then slowly add flour until it forms a flexible, 
    elastic mass, then cover and let the dough rest 30 minutes. When kneading the dough, if the dough is too dry, you should add a little water.

Step 2: Making the inside of the cake:

     - Wood ear mushrooms soak in warm water for about 30 minutes to bloom and then cut into small pieces. Soak the vermicelli in cold water until it expands, then cut it 
    into small pieces. Peel the red onion, cut it into cubes. Chopped kohlrabi or bean sprouts. Quail eggs or hard-boiled chicken eggs, remove the shell.
     - Stir-fry the purple onion, add 200g of ground pork and onion to stir-fry, the other fillings remain the same, do not stir-fry together to keep the sweetness.
     - Then mix the meat with other ingredients: wood ear, vermicelli, tapioca, kohlrabi, season with 1 teaspoon seasoning powder, 1/2 teaspoon pepper.

Step 3: Create pie crust:

     - After incubation for 30 minutes, soak in warm water for about 30 minutes to expand and then cut into small pieces.
     - Replaceable latch handle. Dry powder sprinkled a thin layer on the table surface to prevent sticking, pour the dough out and roll it out very thin. After rolling the dough, 
    you take a bowl bigger than the rice bowl and press it down to form a round mold.

Step 4: Cake steps:

     - Crack an egg, take only the yolk into a bowl, beat it, and have a brush ready to sweep. After making the crust, fold the cake out, brush a thin layer of egg yolk around 
    the edge of the pillowcase, put the filling in the middle, and then put the quail egg in. Slowly fold the edges of the cake back and create a fold.

Step 5: Step of frying the cake:

     - Pour oil into a pan or small pot, with a thick bottom to save oil, remember to deep-fry the surface of the cake with a medium donkey. Fry the pillow cake twice, once 
    until the cake is just cooked, evenly golden. The second time, fry to make the cake crispy.

Step 6: Make delicious pillow cake dipping sauce: 

     - Put the following mixtures in a bowl: 3 tablespoons fish sauce, 2 tablespoons water, 1 teaspoon sugar, garlic, minced chili, mix until sugar dissolves, add 1 teaspoon 
    lemon juice or vinegar, Add kohlrabi, thinly grated carrots.

=> After completing the above steps, you have yourself a delicious cake.
