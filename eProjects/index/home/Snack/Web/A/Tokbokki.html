Food's name: Tokbokki

▸ Ingredients:

  • Rice cake: 500g
  • Chicken broth: 4 cups
  • Korean fish cake: 4 pieces
  • Gochujang Korean Chili Sauce: 1 tablespoon
  • Cabbage, scallion, garlic, paprika,...

▸ Time to prepare: 30 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: 1 large bowl

▸ Detailed recipe: 

     - Fish cake cut into small pieces to eat. Cabbage, de-leaves, and shredded. Onions washed, cut into small pieces. Peeled garlic, minced.
     - Bring the water to a boil, put the rice cakes in, and cook for 5 minutes. Then, take out the rice cakes and drop them in cold water to prevent them from 
    sticking together, then take them out to drain.
     - Mix 1 tablespoon Korean chili sauce, 1 teaspoon chili powder (can be increased if you like spicy), 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 tablespoon water.
     - Boil 4 bowls of prepared broth, then add the mixture above and stir well, skim off any foam if any.
     - Drop the fish cakes in and cook for a few minutes, then continue to add the rice cakes to cook, stirring constantly so that the cakes do not stick to the pot. 
    When the rice cakes are almost thickened, add the cabbage. Tokbokki thickens then turns off the stove.
     - Ladle Tokbokki into bowls, sprinkle with sesame, spring onions, and enjoy while still hot.

=> Now take it out and enjoy !
