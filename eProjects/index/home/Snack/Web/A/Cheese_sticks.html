Food's name: Cheese sticks

▸ Ingredients:

  • Mozzarella cheese: 300g
  • Deep-fried dough: 100g
  • Wheat flour: 100g
  • Chicken eggs: 2 eggs
  • Cooking oil, pepper, seasoning, salt, sugar, chopped parsley

▸ Time to prepare: 30 minutes

▸ Approx quantity: servings for 2 people

▸ Detailed recipe: 

     - Leave the Mozzarella cheese in the refrigerator for about 1 hour and then take it out and cut it into long, bite-sized chunks.
     - Prepare the first bowl with a mixture of 2 beaten eggs, finely chopped parsley, pepper, the second bowl is flour and the third bowl is breadcrumbs. 
    With each bowl, you add a little seasoning to taste.
     - Roll the sliced cheese through three bowls in turn in the order of flour, then beaten eggs, and finally breadcrumbs and place in the freezer for about 
    2 hours to shape this cheese. Then fry in oil with moderate heat until golden brown and crispy.

=> After completing the above steps, you have finished making delicious and greasy cheese sticks, now bring it out and enjoy !
